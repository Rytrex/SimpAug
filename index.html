About

Simple AugNet is an alternative site to Augsburg College's site, Augnet. It only includes important links on AugNet. If there is something you need on AugNet that is not here, please visit their site. This site is made for my personal use, but is available to everyone. If you would like to request something be put on here, feel free to e-mail me at vod@augsburg.edu.

How to Use

To use Simple AugNet, use the two buttons at the top of the page to log into AugNet and Google. From here, you should have full control of AugNet from here so you can close the two tabs the buttons open. If you are having issues, see the Help section.
This site is Mobile Friendly! Try it out on your phone too!

Help

G-mail and Drive are not working properly!
You may have noticed that there are two buttons to log in. One is for AugNet and the other is to log you into the Google side of AugNet. Make sure you use both buttons!
If it is taking you to a different G-Mail account, you probably are logged into more than two Google accounts. I recommend logging into your primary account, then your Augsburg account, then any other accounts you may have.

Both Moodles and Calendar are not working properly!
This is because you are currently not logged into AugNet. Use the two login buttons at the top to make sure you are logged into AugNet and Google.

If you any other questions or would like to report a bug, feel free to e-mail me at vod@augsburg.edu.
